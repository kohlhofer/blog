John Hill has put together [a remarkable overview][1] of housing patterns in the US. Very few–if any–are walkable.

Finding a real community to live in rather than a place to just park your car at night is not as easy as it should be.

Side note: Gated communities appear to be the ultimate in reality denial and disconnect. At least they keep the people on the outside safe from the people who choose that life style.
